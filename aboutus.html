About Us

Cross Canada Views is a comprehensive online photo gallery that was created by several students attending the
Full Stack Web Development program at York University.


Most of the students who developed this website are from the Toronto area, however there are also students from all over Canada!


All of the creators as Canadians think Canada is home to some of the most incredible landscapes and wildlife.

This photo gallery is assembled of 100% copyright free pictures, we encourage you to download them and use in your own projects!

If you'd like to contact the creators, please visit our Contact page.
